It All Starts with Passion

Founded in 1985, Bella Pizza began with a simple dream: to bring authentic Neapolitan pizza to every home. Our founder, Giuseppe Bella, brought his grandmother's secret recipe from Italy.

Fresh Ingredients, Every Time

We source only the finest ingredients: San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella di bufala, and organic flour. Each topping is hand-selected daily from local farmers.

Wood-Fired Perfection

Our traditional wood-fired oven reaches 900°F, cooking each pizza in just 90 seconds. This creates the perfect charred crust and melted cheese you love.

Delivered Hot & Fresh

Within 30 minutes of leaving our oven, your pizza arrives at your door. Our insulated delivery bags ensure every bite is as perfect as intended.
